# 🌍 About Codju

Codju is India's AI Education Partner for Next Generation Schools.

We help schools transform traditional ICT education into a modern AI-powered learning ecosystem through curriculum, technology, robotics, teacher training, and hands-on learning experiences.

Built by IITians, educators, and AI experts, Codju empowers students with the skills required for the future workforce.

### Our Mission

> Empower 2 Million Indian Students with AI, Coding, Robotics, Computational Thinking, and Digital Skills by 2030.
